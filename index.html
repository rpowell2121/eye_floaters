THE SCIENCE OF WHAT CAUSES EYE FLOATERS

Most of your eyes are filled with a jelly like substance known as the vitreous fluid, which contains a lot of collagen proteins. When these proteins become damaged, they clump up and cast shadows on your retina, which appear visually as floaters.

4K Vision uses clinically backed ingredients to prevent the damage to the vitreous fluid and to degrade any existing clumps, keeping your vision clear and free of debris.
